How Community Solar Works

1

Build Solar Farm

A large solar farm is built in a suitable location within the community.

2

Community Subscription

Residents subscribe to a portion of the solar farm's output without installing panels.

3

Generate Electricity

The solar farm generates clean electricity and feeds it into the local grid.

4

Receive Credits

Subscribers receive credits on their electricity bills proportional to their share.

Success Stories

Bhaktapur Community Solar Project

Bhaktapur, Nepal

200 kW System Size
150 Households
15% Average Savings

The Bhaktapur Community Solar Project was launched in 2022 to provide renewable energy access to residents without suitable rooftops. The 200 kW system serves 150 households and has created 10 local maintenance and administration jobs. Participants save an average of 15% on their monthly electricity bills while reducing the community's carbon footprint by approximately 180 tons annually.
